## 1.0 Introduction

This document outlines the standard operating procedure for setting up new phones for mass account creation and engagement across social media and dating platforms. The goal is to create and maintain a network of active and humanized accounts to connect with new prospects and reactivate past high-value buyers.

### Critical Success Factors

- **WiFi must remain OFF at all times** - Use cellular data only

- **All data must be tracked in Google Sheets** - Mandatory for all accounts

- **Human-like behavior patterns are essential** - Randomization is key

- **Rapport building with buyers is the primary objective** - Quality over quantity

## 2.0 Initial Phone Setup

This checklist must be completed for each new phone before any apps are installed.

### 2.1 Setup Checklist

- [ ] **Phone Labeled:** Phone has been physically labeled with a unique ID 

- [ ] **WiFi Disabled:** WiFi is turned OFF in settings (**CRITICAL STEP**)

- [ ] **Location Services Enabled:** Location services are turned ON

- [ ] **App Tracking Disabled:** App tracking is **NEVER** authorized when prompted

- [ ] **Photo Access Granted:** Full access to photo library granted to all relevant apps

- [ ] **Model Folder Transferred:** Model's content folder successfully transferred to phone

- [ ] **Google Sheets Access:** Ensure phone can access Google Sheets for tracking

## 4.0 Humanization & Warming Period

This is the **most critical phase** for account longevity. The goal is to mimic realistic human behavior.

### 4.1 Initial Activity (Immediately After Account Creation)

- **Duration:** 12-20 minutes of continuous human-like activity

- **Activities:** Browse apps, watch videos, scroll feeds, explore content

### 4.2 Ongoing Activity Pattern

- **Duration:** 2 weeks to 1 month minimum

- **Randomization is KEY:** Some days engage heavily, other days don't touch the phone at all

- **Daily Activities:**
  - Browse the internet
  - Watch YouTube videos
  - Scroll social media feeds
  - Like, comment, and share content
  - Open various apps at different times

### 4.3 Reddit-Specific Actions

- **Priority Level:** SUPER IMPORTANT for overall profile credibility

- Engage in relevant subreddits

- Comment on posts

- Upvote/downvote content

- Build karma organically

### 4.4 Humanization Checklist

- [ ] Perform 12-20 mins initial activity

- [ ] Randomize daily usage patterns

- [ ] Engage on Reddit (comments/votes)

- [ ] Watch YouTube videos

- [ ] Scroll social feeds naturally

- [ ] Vary activity timing throughout the day

## 5.0 Target Audience & Outreach Strategy

### 5.1 Target Profile Definition: "Buyers"

**Demographics:**

- Older males

- High net worth individuals

**Interests:**

- Actively follow and engage with specific interest-based communities

- High following count of relevant interest pages

**Platform Behavior:**

- Active on Facebook, Instagram, and other platforms

- Engage with content regularly

### 5.2 Outreach & Engagement Protocol

1. **Identify & Follow:** Send friend requests to individuals matching the Buyer profile

1. **Build Rapport:** Consistently **like their posts** and engage meaningfully with their content

1. **Rate Limiting:**
  - **Weeks 1-2:** 5-10 friend requests/follows per day
  - **Weeks 3-4+:** Gradually increase to 20-30 per day

### 5.3 Engagement Best Practices

- Like posts regularly (not all at once)

- Comment thoughtfully when appropriate

- Share relevant content

- Respond to messages promptly

- Build genuine connections over time

### 5.4 Outreach Checklist

- [ ] Identify target profiles (Buyers)

- [ ] Send 5-10 friend requests/day (Weeks 1-2)

- [ ] Like posts regularly to build rapport

- [ ] Scale to 20-30 requests/day (Weeks 3+)

- [ ] Log all interactions in Google Sheets

### 7.1 Account Creation Schedule

- **Day 1:** Create the **FIRST** TikTok account

- **Day 5:** Create the **SECOND** TikTok account

- **Day 10:** Create the **THIRD** TikTok account

### 7.2 Per-Account Setup

**For Each TikTok Account:**

1. **IP Management:** Use a new IP address for each account creation

1. **Immediate Post:** Post ONE TikTok video **as soon as the account is made**

1. **Delayed Bio:** Add bio **3-5 days AFTER** account creation
  - Bio must be varied (don't use exact same text)
  - Include Snapchat username in bio

1. **Cross-App Tracking:** **ENABLE** TikTok's ability to track across other apps

1. **Ongoing Posts:** Random posting schedule (post one video, miss a couple days, post again)

1. **Google Sheets Logging:** Immediately log username and password

## Troubleshooting & FAQs

### Q: Can I use WiFi if it's a private network?

**A:** No. WiFi must remain OFF at all times. Use cellular data only.

### Q: How often should I post on TikTok?

**A:** After the initial post on Day 0, use a random schedule. Post once, miss a couple days, post again. This simulates organic growth.

### Q: What if a friend request is rejected?

**A:** Log it in the Target Audience Tracker and move on. Continue engaging with other targets.

### Q: Can I use the same bio across all accounts?

**A:** No. Vary the bio text. Include Snapchat username but change wording for each account.

### Q: How long should I wait before messaging a buyer?

**A:** Build rapport first through likes and comments. Wait at least 1-2 weeks before sending a direct message.
